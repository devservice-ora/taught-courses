# Teaching Philosophy
**Jim D. Pham**

---

## Core Philosophy
My goal is to **instill core values and knowledge** in every student, empowering them to understand and impact the world around them. I am firmly convinced that anyone can be taught nearly any subject when provided with the right guidance, patience, and approach. This deep-seated conviction is what drives my own commitment to **lifelong learning** and continuous education, ensuring that my teaching methodologies evolve alongside the fields I instruct.

## Technology Integration
As an educator, I prepare students for a professional landscape driven by digital transformation. I actively integrate **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and the **Internet of Things (IoT)** into the curriculum to:
* **Increase engagement** through interactive, real-world technology.
* **Build industry readiness** for rapidly evolving technical fields.
* **Enhance collaboration** in team-based project environments.

## Pedagogy & Classroom Environment
I teach students **how to solve problems** rather than just providing prescribed answers. 

> **The Classroom Culture:** I cultivate an inclusive, collaborative environment where students freely share ideas, test technical concepts, and engage in active dialogue. 

Continuous, constructive feedback is the cornerstone of my instruction, ensuring students learn through hands-on, iterative problem-solving.
